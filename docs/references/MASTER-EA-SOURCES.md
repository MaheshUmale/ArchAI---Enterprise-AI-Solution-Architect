## 1. Enterprise Architecture Frameworks & Standards

### 1.1 Core Specifications
1. **[TOGAF® Standard, 10th Edition](https://www.opengroup.org/togaf-standard-10th-edition-downloads)**
   - *Authority*: The Open Group
   - *Topic*: Enterprise Architecture Framework
   - *Description*: The fundamental industry standard for enterprise architecture, providing a comprehensive approach for design, planning, implementation, and governance.
2. **[ArchiMate® 3.2 Specification](https://publications.opengroup.org/c220)**
   - *Authority*: The Open Group
   - *Topic*: Modeling Language
   - *Description*: An open and independent modeling language for enterprise architecture that is supported by different tool vendors and consulting firms.
3. **[Zachman Framework Ontology](https://www.zachman.com/)**
   - *Authority*: Zachman International
   - *Topic*: Enterprise Ontology
   - *Description*: A schema for organizing architectural artifacts that takes into account both who the artifact targets and what particular issue is being addressed.
4. **[ISO/IEC/IEEE 42010:2011](https://www.iso.org/standard/50508.html)**
   - *Authority*: ISO/IEC/IEEE
   - *Topic*: Architecture Description
   - *Description*: Defines requirements on the description of system, software, and enterprise architectures.
5. **[The Open Group Library](https://www.opengroup.org/library)**
   - *Authority*: The Open Group
   - *Topic*: Standards Repository
   - *Description*: Central repository for all Open Group standards, guides, and white papers across all domains.

### 1.2 Industry-Specific Frameworks
6. **[BIAN Service Landscape 14.0](https://bian.org/deliverables/service-landscape/)**
   - *Authority*: BIAN
   - *Topic*: Banking Industry Architecture
   - *Description*: A standardized banking service landscape that defines the standard business functional boundaries and information assets for the banking industry.
7. **[TM Forum Open Digital Architecture (ODA)](https://www.tmforum.org/oda/)**
   - *Authority*: TM Forum
   - *Topic*: Telecom Transformation
   - *Description*: A blueprint for a new generation of customer-centric, modular, and cloud-native IT and network systems.
8. **[eTOM Business Process Framework](https://www.tmforum.org/resources/standard/etom-business-process-framework/)**
   - *Authority*: TM Forum
   - *Topic*: Telecom Process Hierarchy
   - *Description*: The industry standard business process framework for the telecommunications and digital service provider industry.
9. **[FEAF (Federal Enterprise Architecture Framework)](https://www.cio.gov/)**
   - *Authority*: US CIO Council
   - *Topic*: Government EA
   - *Description*: Provides a common methodology for information technology acquisition, use, and disposal for the United States Federal Government.
10. **[DoDAF (Department of Defense Architecture Framework)](https://dodcio.defense.gov/Library/DoD-Architecture-Framework/)**
    - *Authority*: US DoD
    - *Topic*: Defense Systems Engineering
    - *Description*: An architecture framework for the United States Department of Defense that provides visualization and data tools for complex systems.
