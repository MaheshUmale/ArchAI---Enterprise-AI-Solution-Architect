TOGAF® Standard, 10th Edition Downloads

The TOGAF Standard, 10th Edition is available to all to download free-of-charge for non-commercial use. That will usually mean using it inside your organization. To use the TOGAF Standard, 10th Edition for commercial purposes, a Commercial License is available.

How do I download the TOGAF Standard, 10th Edition?

Follow the instructions on this page to download the TOGAF Standard, 10th Edition. Once you have registered you can immediately download the TOGAF Standard, 10th Edition.

  • Downloading as a Member of The Open Group
  • Downloading as a Non-Member

Downloading as a Member of The Open Group

If your organization is a Member of The Open Group, you may download and use the TOGAF Standard, 10th Edition internally under a personal Member License. If you wish to distribute the TOGAF Standard, 10th Edition within your organization, see the Commercial and Non-Commercial Licenses below.

The download procedure:

  1. Check the TOGAF Standard, 10th Edition, Member Licensing Conditions; then
  2. Register and download the TOGAF Standard, 10th Edition under a Member License (you will need to supply your existing The Open Group Member ID and password)

Downloading as a Non-Member

You may download the TOGAF Standard, 10th Edition under a free, personal, 90-day Evaluation License; and you may then go on to use it internally under a free, perpetual, Non-Commercial License, if you wish. Alternatively, you may take out an annual Commercial License.

Applying for a TOGAF Standard, 10th Edition, Evaluation License

The download procedure:

  1. Check the TOGAF Standard, 10th Edition Evaluation Licensing Conditions; then
  2. Register and download the TOGAF Standard, 10th Edition under a 90-day Evaluation License

If you download the TOGAF Standard, 10th Edition, you are presumed to have read and accepted the TOGAF Standard, 10th Edition Evaluation Licensing Conditions. There is no need to complete and submit the license.

 

After the Evaluation Period

After expiry of the 90-day evaluation period you should apply for a more permanent License:

  • A free, perpetual Non-Commercial License is available to Corporate and Academic Institutions wanting to use the TOGAF Standard, 10th Edition for non-commercial use
  • An annual Commercial License is available for organizations wishing to exploit the TOGAF Standard, 10th Edition commercially

You are under no obligation to take out any of these licenses. However, if you decline, you will be obliged to send an email confirming that you have removed the TOGAF Standard, 10th Edition licensed materials from your machine.
